Overworld

Walk on grass & forests to encounter monsters. Towns have shops. Use SPACE to enter shops, dungeons, open chests, or fight bosses.

Battle

Click actions: Attack, Spells, Abilities, Items, or Flee. Turn order is decided by initiative rolls.

Progression

Defeat monsters for XP & gold. Level up to learn spells & abilities. Allocate stat points at ASI levels (4, 8, 12, 16, 19).

Tips

• Buy a Dungeon Key in Willowdale to enter crypts.
• Explore to reveal the fog of war.
• Track enemy AC by watching your hit/miss rolls.
• Equip better gear from chests & shops.
