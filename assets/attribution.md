## Recommended Free Asset Sources

### Tilesets & Sprites
- [OpenGameArt.org](https://opengameart.org/) - CC0, CC-BY, GPL licensed assets
- [itch.io](https://itch.io/game-assets/free) - Various free game assets
- [Kenney](https://kenney.nl/assets) - CC0 licensed asset packs
- [LPC (Liberated Pixel Cup)](https://lpc.opengameart.org/) - CC-BY-SA & GPL assets

### Audio
- [Freesound](https://freesound.org/) - Various CC licenses
- [OpenGameArt.org Audio](https://opengameart.org/art-search-advanced?keys=&field_art_type_tid%5B%5D=12) - CC0, CC-BY

### Fonts
- [Google Fonts](https://fonts.google.com/) - Open Font License
- [FontSquirrel](https://www.fontsquirrel.com/) - Free for commercial use

## License Abbreviations

- **CC0**: Public Domain, no attribution required
- **CC-BY**: Attribution required
- **CC-BY-SA**: Attribution required, share-alike
- **GPL**: GNU General Public License
- **OFL**: SIL Open Font License
